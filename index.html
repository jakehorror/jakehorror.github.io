About Acer Management

Acer Management was founded by Jaden Frey, who turned a lifelong passion for fixing things into a thriving business. Starting as an apprentice with a family friend, Jaden honed his skills and developed a deep understanding of quality craftsmanship.

He later branched out to create his own company, bringing together a dedicated team of friends who share his commitment to excellence. Together, they fix, build, and create, tackling every project with a focus on doing the job right the first time.
